All about the "Modern House"

Location: Novoaleksandrovka, Dnepropetrovsk region

Area: 190 m2

Year: 2017

Visualization: Toma Podolianko

In collaboration with our partners TOI group we have developed a modern house Modern House, with a swimming pool for a big family in the village Novoaleksandrivka in the Dnepropetrovsk region. The house has a free lay-out with a beautiful double-light space, connecting the living room and the second floor, with three bedrooms, bathrooms and a wardrobe. On the first floor there is a spacious entrance hall, a bathroom, a laundry room, a cabinet, a living room and a kitchen with a dining room, from which there is an exit to the pool area.
